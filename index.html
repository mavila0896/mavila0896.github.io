Major Decider

Major Decider is a web application that helps students who are in high school trying to figure out what they want to study in college or what they want to do in the future. The logic behind it is by questionaire and results from the questionaire will then be taken and compared from the database of majors and will give you the top 3 majors you will/should fit in well. For example, if I wanted to work with computers and math, the top 3 majors that will be sleected are Applied Math for Computer Science, Computer Engineering, and Computer Science since it fits in the results the person has answered.
